Workflow & Architecture Walkthrough

A "Hedge Fund Analyst" approach to automated trading.

The "Waterfall" Logic

The bot operates on a strict confirmation hierarchy.

A trade must pass through 3 Gates to be executed.

  • Gate 1: High Timeframe Trend (30m & 15m)
  • Gate 2: AI Prediction (5m)
  • Gate 3: Low Timeframe Entry (3m & 1m)

System Architecture

Visualizing the data flow from MT5 to the AI Model and back.

Gate 1: The Big Picture

Determining Market Direction

  • Analyzes 30-minute and 15-minute charts.
  • Uses EMA 50 (Exponential Moving Average).
  • Bullish: Price > EMA 50 on BOTH timeframes.
  • Bearish: Price < EMA 50 on BOTH timeframes.
  • If mixed, the bot stays Neutral (No Trade).

Gate 2: The AI Brain

Predicting the Next Move

  • Uses an LSTM Neural Network (Long Short-Term Memory).
  • Analyzes the last 60 candles on the 5-minute timeframe.
  • Features: Price, RSI, MACD, ATR, Bollinger Bands, SMC.
  • Predicts the Exact Closing Price of the next candle.

Gate 3: The Sniper

Timing the Entry

  • Validates the signal on 3-minute and 1-minute charts.
  • Checks for Momentum Alignment.
  • Prevents buying at the top of a pullback or selling at the bottom.
  • Ensures we enter with the immediate flow of the market.

The decision tree from Start to Execution.

Risk Management

The Safety Net

  • Stop Loss (SL): Placed at recent Swing High/Low (Order Blocks) or 1.5x ATR.
  • Take Profit (TP): Automatically set to 2x Risk (1:2 Risk-Reward).
  • Position Sizing: Dynamically calculated to risk only 1-2% of equity.

Execution & Monitoring

  • MT5 Integration: Direct order placement with SL/TP attached.
  • Dashboard: Real-time visualization of signals and account status.
  • Notifications: WhatsApp alerts for confirmed trades.

Fully automated, robust, and risk-managed.
